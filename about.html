Henry Lewek is a first year student at the University of Hartford and persuing a degree in Human Computer Interaction Design (HCID). Coming from a small town in Bedford, NH, he has a big passion for everything and anything technology related. He has a 21 year old cat named Jasper who matches his fur with Henry's hair. He drives a Mini Cooper which is a manual car. Some of Henry's favorite hobbies are listening to music, keeping up to date with technology, and being social.

Choosing to go to the University of Hartford was an easy choice for him. It was the second college his visited, and fell in love with it instantly. Henry is a big fan of all four seasons so staying in New England was a must for him. Plus with it only being roughly 2 hours away from New Hampshire, and the fantastic campus/opportunities that the college presents, UHART was the perfect fit.
